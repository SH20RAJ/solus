# Solus

## Tagline

**Leave everyone. Don't leave yourself.**

Alternative taglines:

* Live first. Share later.
* The social network for yourself.
* Disconnect from everyone. Reconnect with yourself.
* Your life. Your memories. Your timeline.
* A place to discover yourself.

---

# Vision

Solus is a social platform designed for one person—you.

Today's social media encourages people to document life for an audience. Every photo, story, and post is influenced by likes, comments, followers, and algorithms.

Solus removes the audience.

It gives people a familiar social-media experience where they can post stories, photos, videos, thoughts, and memories exactly as they do on Instagram or Snapchat—but everything is private by default.

The goal isn't to disconnect from life.

It's to disconnect from the pressure of being watched.

---

# The Problem

Modern social media has shifted from self-expression to performance.

People often ask themselves:

* Will this get likes?
* Is this aesthetic enough?
* What will people think?
* Should I post this?

Eventually, many people stop living moments naturally and start creating moments for social media.

Some people even deactivate every social account because they need peace—but then they lose the habit of documenting their lives altogether.

There is no product that lets people continue documenting their lives without an audience.

---

# The Solution

Solus is a personal social network.

Everything works like a familiar social media app, except there is no audience.

Users can:

* Post photos
* Upload videos
* Write captions
* Create stories
* Save memories

Everything remains private.

Nobody can discover the account.

Nobody can follow it.

Nobody can like it.

Nobody can comment.

Nobody can message it.

The only person experiencing the timeline is its owner.

---

# Core Philosophy

Social media made us perform for others.

Solus helps people live for themselves.

---

Someone decides to spend three months in the Himalayas.

They deactivate:

* Instagram
* Facebook
* Snapchat
* X

Instead of losing memories, they open Solus.

Every day they:

* Upload sunrise photos.
* Write about their day.
* Record videos.
* Post stories.
* Capture moments.

Three months later, they return with a complete timeline of their journey—not influenced by anyone else's opinions.

---

# Sharing (Optional)

Everything starts private.

If users later decide their journey could inspire others, they can publish it.

Options include:

* Make a single post public.
* Publish a collection of posts as a journey.
* Make the entire profile public.
* Schedule a future publication date.
* Share a link with specific people.

Until the scheduled publication date, visitors see:

> "This page will become public on [date]."

When the date arrives, the content becomes publicly accessible.

Sharing is always optional.

Privacy is always the default.

---

# MVP

Keep the first version intentionally simple.

Features:

* Sign up
* Private profile
* Create posts
* Upload photos
* Upload videos
* Stories
* Personal timeline
* Edit and delete posts
* Optional public sharing
* Scheduled publishing
* Shareable public links

Nothing else.

No follower system.

No explore page.

No feed.

No messaging.

No notifications designed to increase screen time.

---

# Success Metric

The goal is not to maximize time spent in the app.

The goal is to help people preserve meaningful moments and return to them over time.

If someone opens Solus only a few minutes each day but builds a timeline they treasure for years, the product has succeeded.
