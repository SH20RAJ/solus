# The Problem

Social media promised connection.

Instead, it created performance.

Every photo becomes a decision.

Every moment becomes content.

Every experience is filtered through one question:

> **"What will people think?"**

People don't just share their lives anymore.

They perform them.

Millions of people delete Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, and X every year in search of peace.

But when they leave social media, they also lose something important.

They stop recording their lives.

Their memories disappear into camera rolls, scattered notes, and forgotten folders.

The internet has perfected sharing.

It has never perfected remembering.

---

# Our Belief

People don't need another social network.

They need a place where they can simply exist.
